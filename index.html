What Is Bahasan Perintis?

We are a place for a group of people who are dedicated to exploring and sharing content, information, tips, innovations and other useful things related to startups.

  • Where are the places we share?

    We share on three social media, on Instagram, TikTok, and blog.

  • What is our goal?

    We want to influence people with our positive content, we also made Bahasan Perintis to fulfill the final project of the Digital Marketing course.

We value your support above all else. It drives us to share meaningful insights about startups and fuels our dedication. Thank you for being part of Bahasan Perintis and supporting our mission to highlight and inspire through startup stories.
